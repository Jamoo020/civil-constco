## Project overview

Comprehensive coastal road upgrade including shore-protection measures, improved drainage, and resurfacing across 28km of arterial routes serving Mombasa's eastern corridor.

## Scope of works

- Earthworks and pavement rehabilitation
- Drainage and culvert installation
- Coastal protection revetments
- Traffic management and safety improvements

## Impact

Improved resilience against coastal erosion, reduced travel times, and enhanced connectivity for port-access communities.
